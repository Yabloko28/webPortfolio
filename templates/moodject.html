Moodject

Moodject is a mobile app for creative people, who are in constant need to document their inspiration, organize and brainstorm their ideas. The format of the mobile app allows to access this tool at any time, so the users are not limited in putting down their immediate thoughts, ideas and inspiration, whenever they feel like doing so.

The app is supposed to be treated like a working space, so the design has to be very clear and minimal, resembling a clean working space with all the necessary tools that are understandable and easy to use.
